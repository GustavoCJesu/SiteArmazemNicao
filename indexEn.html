A little about us

Armazéns Gerais Nicão is a reference in storage and logistics services in the Andradas (MG) region. Founded in January 2019, the company specializes in offering complete storage solutions in logistics warehouses, focusing on efficiency, security and personalized service for each client.



Strategically located on the Andradas / Espírito Santo do Pinhal highway, in the Contendas district, Nicão acts as an important partner for producers, distributors and companies seeking proper infrastructure to store goods in an organized and safe way.

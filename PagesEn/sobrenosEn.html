A Little About Our History

Armazém Nicão is a reference in storage and logistics in Andradas (MG), operating as a strategic point for the custody, organization and handling of goods in the region. With an adequate infrastructure and a privileged location, it serves companies and producers with security and efficiency. Throughout its trajectory, Armazém Nicão has consolidated itself as a reliable partner in the local logistics chain, contributing to the distribution and preservation of goods from different sectors. Always attentive to market demands, it continues investing in infrastructure and continuous improvement of its services.

Our Mission, Vision and Values

To offer complete solutions in coffee storage, processing and logistics, ensuring quality, security and efficiency at every stage of the process, adding value to our clients' production through close and reliable service.

To be a regional reference in infrastructure and services for the coffee sector, recognized for operational excellence, applied technology and the trust built with producers and partners over time.

To act with quality and responsibility at every stage of coffee storage and handling, prioritizing safety, operational efficiency and commitment to each client, always with transparency and trust.
